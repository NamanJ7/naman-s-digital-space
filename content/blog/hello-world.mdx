I finally rebuilt my personal site. The old one had served me well, but it
had quietly turned into a place I never updated. This time I wanted something
I'd actually enjoy returning to — a home for my work, my writing, and the
occasional half-formed idea.

## What this site is

A few things live here:

- **About** — the short version of who I am and what I'm up to.
- **Portfolio** — software, research, and consulting projects worth showing.
- **Blog** — this newsletter, where I think out loud.
- **Contact** — the easiest way to reach me.

## What I'm aiming for

> Write the post you wish you'd found when you started.

That's the bar. Fewer, better posts. Things I'd want to read a year from now.
If any of it is useful to you, even better.

More soon.
